Who We Are

Tied by Hand. Trusted on the Water.

Amiani's Fishing Flies started as a passion project — late evenings at a tying bench, experimenting with feathers, thread, and hook until patterns emerged that truly moved like life beneath the surface.

What began as flies tied for personal use quickly caught the attention of fellow anglers who could see the difference quality makes. Today, we hand-tie every single fly with the same care and attention that went into that very first pattern.

Based in Kenya, we draw inspiration from the rivers, lakes, and streams that surround us — and we're proud to bring that local knowledge to anglers across the country and beyond.
